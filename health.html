How Pollution Affects Health

🌫 Air Pollution

Causes asthma, bronchitis, lung cancer, and cardiovascular diseases.

💧 Water Pollution

Leads to waterborne diseases, digestive disorders, and toxin exposure.

🔊 Noise Pollution

Causes stress, sleep disturbances, hearing loss, and hypertension.

Respiratory Health & Pollution

Long-term exposure to polluted air increases the risk of chronic respiratory diseases. Children and elderly individuals are especially vulnerable. Sustainable environmental policies and public awareness are essential to reduce health risks.

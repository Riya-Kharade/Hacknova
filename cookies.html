Cookies Policy

Learn how EcoPulse uses cookies to improve your experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve website functionality, remember preferences, and analyze usage patterns.

2. How EcoPulse Uses Cookies

  • To improve website performance and loading speed.
  • To remember user preferences (such as theme or visualization settings).
  • To analyze basic traffic patterns for improving user experience.
  • To ensure secure and smooth navigation between pages.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help analyze how users interact with the platform.
  • Preference Cookies: Store user customization settings.

4. User Control & Management

Users can control or disable cookies through their browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality and performance of the website.

5. Third-Party Services

EcoPulse may integrate third-party libraries such as Three.js or Chart.js. These tools may use minimal storage mechanisms required for performance but do not collect personal data directly through this platform.

6. Updates to This Policy

This Cookies Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or project improvements. Continued use of EcoPulse indicates acceptance of any updates.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how EcoPulse handles and protects your data.

1. Information We Collect

EcoPulse is primarily an educational and visualization platform. We do not collect personal data unless explicitly provided by the user.

  • Basic usage data (such as page visits)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Optional user preferences (if stored locally)

2. How We Use Information

  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To analyze general usage patterns
  • To enhance accessibility and responsiveness
  • To maintain platform security

3. Data Storage & Protection

EcoPulse does not store sensitive personal information. Any temporary data (such as preferences) may be stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time.

We follow best practices to ensure the platform remains secure and protected from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

EcoPulse may integrate trusted third-party libraries such as Three.js and Chart.js for visualization purposes. These libraries operate within the browser and do not collect personal user information directly through this platform.

5. User Rights

  • You have the right to clear browser-stored data.
  • You can disable cookies via browser settings.
  • You may choose not to use the platform at any time.

6. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements or platform improvements. Continued use of EcoPulse indicates acceptance of updates.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the EcoPulse development team through the official GitHub repository.
